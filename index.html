About Us

Hi, I'm Jeff.

I am the founder and owner of Hazel River Landscapes. I have over 30 years of experience as a landscaper. It has always been my passion to create beautiful gardens. I take a lot of pride in what I do, and it shows in my work. My specialty is small to medium scale residential garden design.
